Exploring Our Solar System

Introduction

Greetings Earthlings! Did you now our solar system is made up of 8 planets with the sun in the center? The planets are Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune.

Task

Find out which planet is the largest planet.

Find out which planet is the smallest.

Find out which planets are gaseous.

Find out which planets are terrestrial.

Process

  1. Go to planetsforkids.org.
  2. Click on Planets.
  3. Click on Gas Giants and write down the names of the gaseous planets.
  4. Click on Terrestrial Planets and write down the names of the terrestrial planets.
  5. Click on each planet name and read about each planet to determine which planet is largest and which is smallest.
